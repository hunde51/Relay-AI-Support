Billing Policy — RelayAI Support

1. All plans are billed monthly or annually in advance.
2. Refunds are available within 7 days of initial purchase only.
3. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.
4. Failed payments result in a 3-day grace period before account suspension.
5. Annual plans receive a 20% discount compared to monthly billing.
6. Invoices are sent automatically to the billing email on file.
7. To update payment method, go to Settings > Billing > Payment Method.
